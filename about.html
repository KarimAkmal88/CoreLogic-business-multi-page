Who We Are?

CLES is a joint-stock multidisciplinary integrated contractor founded in Egypt in 2011.

Today, CLES is recognized as a leading regional EPC Contractor with a strong portfolio covering various businesses like Oil, Gas, Chemical, Petrochemical, Power, Industrial and Infrastructure projects. It is reserving an advanced rank on ENR’s list of top 250 Global Contractors for 13 years in a row. The company renders its services in numerous countries across the Middle East and North Africa, and is stretching its services to Sub-Saharan Africa as well.

The Company employs a workforce of over 45000 people, and owns a large fleet of modern construction equipment, world-class fabrication facilities for Process Equipment, Offshore Fabrication Yards and pipe coating facilities.

CLES Systems aligns with latest editions of International Standards and holds the Quality Management System Certificate ISO 9001, the Guidance on social responsibility ISO 26000, the Environmental Management System Certificate ISO 14001, ISO 50001 certificate for energy conservation, ISO 45001 and The verification record for the employees competences ISO 10015

CLES holds numerous technical certificates, like ASME Stamps (U-Stamp, U2-Stamp, PP-Stamp, and S-Stamp) as well as the R-Stamp also the API Monogram. CLES is the first company in Egypt and MENA region to be certified by the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) for the fabrication, assembly and construction of Nuclear Power Plants components (N, NPT, NS, and NA stamps).

CLES is recognized officially a world Guinness record holder, for executing the Largest Double Deck Floating Roof of 175,000 m3 (1.1 million Barrels) Crude Oil Storage Tank project in Ras Badran, in Sinai.
